What are you REALLY paying for?

How is your money divided when purcahsing retail and luxury floral arrangments?

You may notice a distinct difference in pricing when comparing retail floral prices to those of luxury wedding and event floral prices. There is a good reason for this distintion and we'll go over that today and what all goes into your pricing depending on what you are after.

First you have to consider the types of bouquets, such as...

  • Hand wrapped bouquet
  • Centerpiece
  • Statement arrangement/ Alter piece
  • Boutonniere

So lets compare!

Retail Shop Wedding/Event Studio
Bout= $15 Bout= $25
Centerpiece= $100 Centerpiece= $350
Handwrap= $150 Handwrap= $300

When you go to a retail shop for your flowers, you are relying on what the company has already purchased within their budget at wholesale cost, and thus there is no risk on their end. They also tend to charge only 25% labor cost for any given arrangement, where as a wedding/event studio will special order your (more than likely) luxury flowers that will cost more wholesale, and they are taking on the risk of you canceling your order. They also tend to be much more difficult type arrangements and so they will charge no less than a 50% labor fee
